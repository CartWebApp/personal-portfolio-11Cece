Hi! I'm Celine — An artist and designer focused on human figures and free spirited designs of digital and traditional media. From where I want to be 5 years from now is to be in college at Fresno State studying my major in graphic design.

CART Reflection

CART has been a great experience for me as it has allowed me to explore my interests and develop new skills. I have learned how to understand and use HTML, CSS, and Javascript to program websites, which has been a valuable tool for showcasing my work. I have also had the opportunity to collaborate with other students and reach out to my instructors and receive feedback on my projects, which has helped me grow as a designer throughout 2026. In 2024-2025 when I took Graphic Design at CART, I learned how to properly use Adobe programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Animate, and to create logo advertisements, infographics, animations, and pursuasive media. Overall, CART has been an enriching experience that has helped me gain confidence in my abilities and prepare for my future career in graphic design.
